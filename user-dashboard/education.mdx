## Overview

Education modules provide learning content to help you understand your server, community guidelines, and topics.

## Module Types

- **Server Orientation**: Welcome guides and rules
- **Tutorials**: Step-by-step guides
- **Resources**: Helpful links and materials

## How It Works

1. **Select Module**: Choose a module to start
2. **Read Content**: Go through the material
3. **Complete Quizzes**: Answer questions (if any)
4. **Mark Complete**: Finish the module
5. **Earn Rewards**: Get XP or badges

## Module Content

Modules may include:
- Text explanations
- Images and diagrams
- Videos
- Interactive quizzes

## Rewards

Completing modules may reward:
- Bonus XP
- Achievement badges
- Special roles
- Access to additional content

## Tips

- Follow recommended learning paths
- Take your time to understand content
- You can revisit completed modules anytime
