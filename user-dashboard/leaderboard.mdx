## Overview

The Leaderboard shows rankings of all server members based on XP, level, and activity metrics. Compete with other members and track your position on the server.

## Leaderboard Features

### Ranking Display

The leaderboard displays:

- **Rank**: Your position (1st, 2nd, 3rd, etc.)
- **Username**: Member's Discord username
- **Avatar**: Profile picture
- **Total XP**: Accumulated experience points
- **Level**: Current level
- **Change**: Position change (↑ or ↓)

### Filtering Options

Filter leaderboards by:

- **All Time**: Total XP since joining
- **This Month**: XP earned this month
- **This Week**: XP earned this week
- **Today**: XP earned today

### Sorting Options

Sort by:

- **XP**: Total experience points (default)
- **Level**: Current level
- **Messages**: Total messages sent
- **Activity**: Recent activity score

## Understanding Rankings

### How Rankings Work

1. **XP-Based**: Primary ranking is by total XP
2. **Tie Breaking**: If XP is equal, level is used
3. **Real-Time Updates**: Rankings update as members earn XP
4. **Server-Specific**: Each server has its own leaderboard

### Your Position

- **Current Rank**: Your position among all members
- **Rank Change**: How your position changed recently
- **XP Behind**: How much XP you need to move up
- **XP Ahead**: How much XP you have over the next person

## Leaderboard Sections

### Top 10

The top 10 members are prominently displayed with:

- Special highlighting for top 3
- Detailed stats for each member
- Quick view of their activity

### Your Position

Your current position is highlighted with:

- Your rank number
- XP and level information
- Nearby competitors (above and below you)

### Full List

Scrollable list showing:

- All server members
- Pagination for large servers
- Search functionality

## Competitive Features

### Rank Badges

Special badges for:

- **#1**: Top member badge
- **Top 10**: Elite member badge
- **Top 25**: Active member badge

### Position Tracking

Track your progress:

- **Historical Position**: See how your rank has changed
- **Rank Goals**: Set targets for your desired rank
- **Progress Indicators**: Visual feedback on rank changes

## Privacy

- **Public Rankings**: Leaderboards are visible to all server members
- **Opt-Out**: Contact server admin if you want to opt out (if available)
- **Anonymous Mode**: Some servers may offer anonymous rankings
