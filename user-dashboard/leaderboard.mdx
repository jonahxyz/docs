## Overview

The leaderboard shows rankings of all server members based on XP and activity.

## What You'll See

- **Rank**: Your position (1st, 2nd, 3rd, etc.)
- **Username & Avatar**: Member information
- **Total XP**: Experience points
- **Level**: Current level
- **Rank Change**: If you moved up or down

## Filtering

View leaderboards by:
- **All Time**: Total XP since joining
- **This Month**: XP earned this month
- **This Week**: XP earned this week
- **Today**: XP earned today

## Your Position

- See your current rank highlighted
- View members above and below you
- Track how much XP you need to move up

## Tips

- Complete challenges to boost your ranking quickly
- Stay active daily for consistent XP gains
- Engage in all channels to maximize opportunities
