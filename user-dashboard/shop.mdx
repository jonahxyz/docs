## Overview

The XP Shop lets you spend your accumulated XP on Discord roles, special perks, and custom rewards.

## Shop Items

### Discord Roles
Purchase roles that give you:
- Special permissions
- Custom name colors
- Access to exclusive channels

### Perks
- **XP Multipliers**: Earn XP faster
- **Custom Badges**: Display special badges
- **Priority Support**: Faster help

### Custom Rewards
Server-specific items created by admins

## How to Buy

1. Browse available items
2. Click an item to see details
3. Check you have enough XP
4. Click "Buy" to purchase
5. Receive item instantly

## Purchase History

View your past purchases:
- Items you've bought
- XP you've spent
- Active items you currently have

## Tips

- Save XP for valuable items
- Check regularly for new items
- Read descriptions carefully before buying
