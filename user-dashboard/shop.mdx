## Overview

The XP Shop allows you to spend your accumulated XP on various rewards including Discord roles, special perks, and custom items created by server admins.

## Shop Features

### Browse Items

The shop displays all available items with:

- **Item Name**: What the item is
- **Description**: Details about the item
- **Price**: XP cost
- **Availability**: Whether it's in stock
- **Category**: Type of item (role, perk, etc.)

### Item Categories

#### Discord Roles

Purchase Discord roles that:

- Give you special permissions
- Change your name color
- Show your status in the server
- Provide access to exclusive channels

#### Perks

Special perks such as:

- **XP Multipliers**: Boost your XP earning rate
- **Custom Badges**: Display special badges
- **Priority Support**: Faster support responses
- **Exclusive Access**: Access to special features

#### Custom Rewards

Server-specific rewards:

- Custom items defined by admins
- Special privileges
- One-time bonuses
- Event participation

## Purchasing Items

### How to Purchase

1. **Browse Shop**: View available items
2. **Select Item**: Click on an item to see details
3. **Check Balance**: Ensure you have enough XP
4. **Purchase**: Click "Buy" to purchase
5. **Instant Delivery**: Receive item immediately

### Purchase Requirements

- **Sufficient XP**: Must have enough XP for the item
- **Availability**: Item must be in stock
- **Prerequisites**: Some items may require certain levels or roles
- **One-Time**: Some items can only be purchased once

## Managing Purchases

### Purchase History

View your purchase history:

- **All Purchases**: Complete list of items bought
- **Purchase Date**: When you bought each item
- **Price Paid**: XP spent
- **Status**: Whether item is still active

### Active Items

See items you currently have:

- **Active Roles**: Discord roles you've purchased
- **Active Perks**: Perks currently in effect
- **Expiration**: When items expire (if applicable)

## Earning XP for Shop

Ways to earn XP to spend in the shop:

- **Daily Activity**: Regular messaging and engagement
- **Challenge Completion**: Bonus XP from challenges
- **Voice Activity**: Time in voice channels
- **Reactions**: Reacting to messages
- **Special Events**: XP bonuses during events

## Item Availability

### Stock Limits

Some items have:

- **Limited Stock**: Only available in limited quantities
- **Time Limits**: Available for a limited time
- **Restocking**: Items may be restocked periodically

### Exclusive Items

Special items that are:

- **One-Time Only**: Can only be purchased once
- **Limited Edition**: Available for special events
- **Level-Locked**: Require certain levels to purchase

## Refunds

- **No Refunds**: XP spent is generally non-refundable
- **Contact Admin**: For issues, contact server administrators
- **Item Issues**: Report problems with purchased items
