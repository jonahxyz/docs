## Overview

Challenges are special tasks created by server admins that reward members with bonus XP and sometimes special rewards upon completion.

## Challenge Types

### Message Challenges

- **Send X Messages**: Reach a message count goal
- **Daily Message Goal**: Send messages every day
- **Message Streak**: Maintain consecutive days of messaging

### Reaction Challenges

- **React X Times**: Add reactions to messages
- **Reaction Variety**: Use different reaction types

### Voice Challenges

- **Voice Time**: Spend time in voice channels
- **Voice Streak**: Join voice channels daily

### Social Challenges

- **Invite Friends**: Bring new members to the server
- **Engagement**: Interact with specific channels or topics

### Custom Challenges

Admins can create custom challenges for:

- Specific actions
- Milestone achievements
- Event participation
- Special occasions

## Viewing Challenges

### Active Challenges

See all currently available challenges with:

- **Challenge Name**: What you need to do
- **Description**: Detailed instructions
- **Reward**: XP and other rewards offered
- **Progress**: Your current progress
- **Time Remaining**: How long the challenge is active

### Your Challenges

View challenges you're working on:

- **In Progress**: Challenges you've started
- **Progress Bar**: Visual progress indicator
- **Completion Status**: How close you are to finishing

### Completed Challenges

History of challenges you've finished:

- **Completion Date**: When you finished
- **Rewards Earned**: What you received
- **Achievement Badge**: Visual confirmation

## Completing Challenges

### How It Works

1. **Automatic Tracking**: Progress is tracked automatically
2. **Real-Time Updates**: Progress updates as you complete actions
3. **Instant Rewards**: Rewards are given immediately upon completion
4. **Notification**: You'll be notified when you complete a challenge

### Challenge Progress

- **Progress Bar**: Visual indicator of completion
- **Current/Goal**: See exactly how much you've done vs. required
- **Time Remaining**: Countdown for time-limited challenges

## Challenge Rewards

### XP Rewards

- **Bonus XP**: Extra XP beyond normal activity
- **One-Time**: Rewarded once per challenge
- **Scaling**: Some challenges offer more XP for higher achievements

### Special Rewards

Some challenges may offer:

- **Discord Roles**: Special roles for completion
- **Shop Credits**: Extra XP for the shop
- **Badges**: Achievement badges
- **Custom Rewards**: Server-specific rewards

## Challenge Notifications

You'll receive notifications for:

- **New Challenges**: When admins create new challenges
- **Progress Updates**: Significant progress milestones
- **Completion**: When you complete a challenge
- **Time Warnings**: When challenges are about to expire

## Challenge History

View your challenge history to see:

- **All Completed**: Every challenge you've finished
- **Completion Rate**: Percentage of challenges completed
- **Total Rewards**: XP and rewards earned from challenges
- **Achievements**: Special accomplishments
