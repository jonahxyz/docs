## Overview

Challenges are special tasks that reward you with bonus XP and sometimes special rewards.

## Challenge Types

- **Message Challenges**: Send X messages
- **Daily Goals**: Send messages every day
- **Voice Challenges**: Spend time in voice channels
- **Reaction Challenges**: Add reactions to messages
- **Social Challenges**: Invite friends or engage with community

## How It Works

1. **View Challenges**: See all available challenges
2. **Start Working**: Your progress is tracked automatically
3. **Complete**: Finish the challenge goal
4. **Get Rewards**: Receive XP and rewards instantly

## Challenge Rewards

- **Bonus XP**: Extra XP beyond normal activity
- **Special Roles**: Discord roles (sometimes)
- **Badges**: Achievement badges
- **Shop Credits**: Extra XP for the shop

## Tips

- Check challenges regularly for new opportunities
- Focus on time-limited challenges first
- Many challenges can be completed alongside normal activity
