## Overview

Your dashboard shows your XP, level, rank, and activity stats at a glance.

## What You'll See

### XP Stats
- **Total XP**: Your accumulated experience points
- **Current Level**: Your level in the server
- **Progress Bar**: How close you are to the next level

### Activity Stats
- **Messages Sent**: Total messages you've sent
- **Reactions**: Reactions you've added
- **Voice Time**: Time spent in voice channels
- **Daily Streak**: Consecutive days active

### Quick Sections
- **Recent Activity**: Latest XP gains and achievements
- **Active Challenges**: Challenges you can complete
- **Voting Campaigns**: Current votes (if available)

## How XP Works

You earn XP automatically for:
- Sending messages (15-25 XP each)
- Reacting to messages
- Time in voice channels
- Completing challenges (bonus XP)

## Leveling Up

- Your level increases as you earn XP
- You'll get a notification when you level up
- Server admins may assign special roles at certain levels

## Tips

- Stay active daily to maintain streaks
- Complete challenges for bonus XP
- Join voice channels for extra XP
- Check the leaderboard to see your rank
