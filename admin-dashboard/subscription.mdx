## Overview

The Subscription page allows you to view your current plan, manage billing, upgrade or downgrade, and see what features are available in each tier.

## Subscription Plans

### Starter Plan

For small servers (0-50 members):

- XP & Level tracking
- Public leaderboards
- Basic challenges
- Activity feed
- XP shop system

### Growth Plan

For growing servers (50-250 members):

- Everything in Starter
- Voting campaigns
- Education modules
- Advanced analytics
- Priority support

### Enterprise Plan

For large servers (250-1000+ members):

- Everything in Growth
- API access
- Custom integrations
- Dedicated support
- Custom features

## Managing Subscription

### Current Plan

View your current subscription:

- **Plan Name**: Your current plan
- **Billing Cycle**: Monthly or annual
- **Next Billing Date**: When next payment is due
- **Features**: What's included in your plan

### Upgrading

Upgrade to a higher plan:

- **Select Plan**: Choose new plan
- **Review Changes**: See what you'll get
- **Confirm Payment**: Complete upgrade
- **Instant Activation**: Features available immediately

### Downgrading

Downgrade to a lower plan:

- **Select Plan**: Choose lower plan
- **Effective Date**: When change takes effect
- **Feature Changes**: What you'll lose
- **Confirm**: Complete downgrade

## Billing

### Payment Methods

Manage payment:

- **Add Card**: Add payment method
- **Update Card**: Change payment details
- **Billing History**: View past invoices
- **Download Invoices**: Get invoice PDFs

### Billing History

View past payments:

- **Invoices**: All past invoices
- **Payment Dates**: When payments were made
- **Amounts**: What you paid
- **Status**: Payment status

## Feature Comparison

Compare plans to see:

- **Feature Matrix**: Side-by-side comparison
- **What's Included**: Features per plan
- **Limitations**: Plan-specific limits
- **Add-ons**: Optional features
