## Overview

Create and manage items that members can purchase with their accumulated XP.

## Creating Shop Items

### Item Types
- **Discord Roles**: Roles members can buy
- **Perks**: XP multipliers, badges, priority support
- **Custom Rewards**: Server-specific items

### Configuration
- **Name & Description**: What the item is
- **Price**: XP cost
- **Category**: Item type
- **Stock**: Available quantity (if limited)
- **Requirements**: Level or role prerequisites

## Managing Items

- Edit item details
- Disable or delete items
- Update stock levels
- View sales analytics

## Shop Analytics

Track:
- Sales count
- Total XP earned
- Most popular items
- Sales trends

## Tips

- Offer variety at different price points
- Write clear descriptions
- Price items fairly
- Add new items regularly
