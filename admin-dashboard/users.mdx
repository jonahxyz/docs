## Overview

View all server members, manage their XP, assign roles, and monitor activity.

## Member List

### Viewing Members
- **Search**: Find members by username
- **Sort**: By XP, level, messages, or activity
- **Filter**: By role, level range, or status

### Member Information
- Username and avatar
- Total XP and level
- Message count and rank
- Join date and last active

## Member Actions

### XP Management
- Add or remove XP
- Set XP to specific amount
- Bulk adjustments for multiple members

### Role Management
- Assign or remove Discord roles
- Auto-assign based on level
- Custom role assignments

## Member Analytics

View detailed stats:
- Activity timeline
- Message history
- XP gains over time
- Challenge progress
- Shop purchases
