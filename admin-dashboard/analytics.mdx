## Overview

Comprehensive analytics to understand your server's growth, engagement, and member activity.

## Key Metrics

### Server Growth
- Member growth over time
- Growth rate and trends
- Retention rates

### Engagement
- Daily, weekly, monthly active users
- Engagement rates
- Activity patterns

### Activity Analysis
- Message volume by time period
- Peak activity times
- Channel activity breakdown
- Voice participation

### XP Distribution
- How XP is spread across members
- Top earners
- Average XP per member

## Time Periods

View analytics for:
- Today
- This week
- This month
- All time
- Custom date range

## Challenge & Shop Analytics

- Challenge completion rates
- Shop sales and popular items
- XP distribution from challenges

## Export

Download analytics as:
- CSV files
- PDF reports
- Custom reports
