## Overview

The Server Support page helps you manage your support team, track support ticket activity, and monitor support team performance.

## Support Team Management

### Adding Support Members

Assign support roles:

- **Select Members**: Choose Discord members
- **Support Roles**: Assign support roles
- **Permissions**: Set support permissions
- **Team Organization**: Organize support team

### Support Activity Tracking

Monitor support activity:

- **Ticket Stats**: Support tickets handled
- **Response Times**: Average response times
- **Resolution Rates**: Tickets resolved
- **Team Performance**: Individual and team stats

## Support Analytics

Track support metrics:

- **Ticket Volume**: Number of tickets
- **Response Times**: How quickly tickets are handled
- **Satisfaction**: Member satisfaction (if tracked)
- **Team Workload**: Distribution of work
