## Overview

Education modules provide learning content to help you understand your server, guidelines, and topics.

## Module Types

- **Server Orientation**: Welcome guides and rules
- **Tutorials**: Step-by-step guides
- **Resources**: Helpful links and materials

## How It Works

1. Select a module
2. Read through the content
3. Complete quizzes (if any)
4. Mark as complete
5. Earn XP or badges

## Module Content

Modules may include:
- Text explanations
- Images and diagrams
- Videos
- Interactive quizzes
- Links to resources

## Learning Paths

Some servers organize modules into:
- Sequential paths
- Prerequisites
- Recommended order

## Rewards

Completing modules may reward:
- Bonus XP
- Achievement badges
- Special roles
- Access to additional content

## Tips

- Follow recommended learning paths
- Take your time to understand
- You can revisit completed modules
