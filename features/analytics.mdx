## Overview

Analytics provide insights into your server's growth, member engagement, and activity patterns.

## Key Metrics

### Server Growth
- Member growth over time
- Growth rates
- Retention rates

### Engagement
- Daily, weekly, monthly active users
- Engagement rates
- Activity patterns

### Activity Analysis
- Message volume
- Peak activity times
- Channel activity
- Voice participation

### XP Distribution
- How XP is spread
- Top earners
- Average XP per member

## Time Periods

View analytics for:
- Today
- This week
- This month
- All time
- Custom date ranges

## Challenge & Shop Analytics

- Challenge completion rates
- Shop sales and popular items
- XP distribution from challenges

## Export

Download analytics as:
- CSV files
- PDF reports
- Custom reports

## Tips

- Review analytics regularly
- Compare different time periods
- Identify trends and patterns
- Use insights to improve your server
