## Overview

NovaXP provides comprehensive analytics to help server admins understand their community's growth, engagement patterns, and member activity.

## Analytics Features

### Server Growth

Track growth metrics:

- **Member Growth**: New members over time
- **Growth Rate**: Percentage growth
- **Retention Rate**: How many stay active
- **Growth Trends**: Visual growth charts

### Engagement Metrics

Measure engagement:

- **Daily Active Users**: Active each day
- **Weekly Active Users**: Active each week
- **Monthly Active Users**: Active each month
- **Engagement Rate**: Percentage of active members

### Activity Analysis

Analyze activity:

- **Message Volume**: Messages per time period
- **Activity Patterns**: Peak activity times
- **Channel Activity**: Activity by channel
- **Voice Activity**: Voice participation

### XP Distribution

Understand XP distribution:

- **Distribution Charts**: How XP is spread
- **Top Earners**: Highest XP earners
- **Average XP**: Average per member
- **XP Growth**: Total XP growth

## Time-Based Analysis

View analytics by period:

- **Today**: Current day
- **This Week**: Last 7 days
- **This Month**: Last 30 days
- **All Time**: Complete history
- **Custom Range**: Select dates

## Channel Analytics

Per-channel statistics:

- **Most Active**: Channels with most activity
- **Channel Growth**: Growth per channel
- **Channel Engagement**: Engagement rates

## Member Analytics

Individual insights:

- **Top Members**: Highest XP earners
- **Most Active**: Most active members
- **New Members**: Recently joined
- **Inactive Members**: Not recently active

## Challenge Analytics

Track challenge performance:

- **Completion Rates**: Percentage completing
- **Popular Challenges**: Most completed
- **Challenge Impact**: Effect on engagement
- **Reward Distribution**: XP from challenges

## Shop Analytics

Analyze shop activity:

- **Total Sales**: Items purchased
- **Popular Items**: Most purchased
- **XP Spent**: Total XP spent
- **Sales Trends**: Sales over time

## Export and Reports

Export analytics:

- **CSV Export**: Download as CSV
- **PDF Reports**: Generate PDFs
- **Custom Reports**: Create specific reports
