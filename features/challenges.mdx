## Overview

Challenges are special tasks that reward bonus XP and sometimes special rewards when completed.

## Challenge Types

- **Message Challenges**: Send X messages
- **Daily Goals**: Send messages every day
- **Voice Challenges**: Spend time in voice
- **Reaction Challenges**: Add reactions
- **Social Challenges**: Invite friends or engage

## How It Works

1. View available challenges
2. Your progress is tracked automatically
3. Complete the challenge goal
4. Receive XP and rewards instantly

## Challenge Rewards

- **Bonus XP**: Extra XP beyond normal activity
- **Discord Roles**: Special roles (sometimes)
- **Badges**: Achievement badges
- **Custom Rewards**: Server-specific rewards

## Tips

- Check challenges regularly
- Focus on time-limited challenges first
- Many challenges work alongside normal activity
