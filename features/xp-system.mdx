## Overview

NovaXP automatically tracks your activity and rewards you with XP, levels, and rewards.

## How You Earn XP

XP is awarded automatically for:
- **Messages**: 15-25 XP per message
- **Reactions**: Reacting to messages
- **Voice Activity**: Time in voice channels
- **Challenges**: Bonus XP from completing challenges

## Leveling Up

- Your level increases as you earn XP
- You'll be notified when you level up
- Server admins may assign special roles at certain levels
- Higher levels may require more XP

## Level Rewards

Server admins can configure:
- Automatic role assignments at specific levels
- Different roles for different levels
- Examples: Level 5 = Member, Level 10 = Active, Level 25 = Veteran

## XP Multipliers

XP can be multiplied by:
- **Channel Multipliers**: Different rates per channel
- **XP Events**: Temporary 2x, 3x events
- **Shop Items**: XP multiplier items you can buy

## Tips

- Stay active daily for consistent XP
- Complete challenges for bonus XP
- Engage in all channels
- Use voice channels for extra XP
