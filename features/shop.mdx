## Overview

The XP Shop allows members to spend their accumulated XP on various rewards including Discord roles, special perks, and custom items created by server admins.

## Shop Items

### Discord Roles

Purchase roles that provide:

- **Special Permissions**: Access to exclusive channels
- **Name Colors**: Custom name colors
- **Status Indicators**: Show your status
- **Exclusive Access**: Access to special features

### Perks

Special perks such as:

- **XP Multipliers**: Boost your XP earning rate
- **Custom Badges**: Display special badges
- **Priority Support**: Faster support responses
- **Exclusive Features**: Access to premium features

### Custom Rewards

Server-specific rewards:

- **Custom Items**: Items defined by admins
- **Special Privileges**: Unique perks
- **One-Time Bonuses**: Single-use rewards
- **Event Items**: Special event rewards

## Purchasing Items

### How to Buy

1. **Browse Shop**: View available items
2. **Select Item**: Click to see details
3. **Check Balance**: Ensure you have enough XP
4. **Purchase**: Click "Buy" to purchase
5. **Instant Delivery**: Receive item immediately

### Purchase Requirements

- **Sufficient XP**: Must have enough XP
- **Availability**: Item must be in stock
- **Prerequisites**: May require certain levels/roles
- **One-Time**: Some items can only be bought once

## Managing Purchases

### Purchase History

View your purchases:

- **All Purchases**: Complete purchase list
- **Purchase Date**: When you bought each item
- **Price Paid**: XP spent
- **Status**: Whether item is still active

### Active Items

See items you currently have:

- **Active Roles**: Roles you've purchased
- **Active Perks**: Perks currently in effect
- **Expiration**: When items expire (if applicable)

## Earning XP for Shop

Ways to earn XP:

- **Daily Activity**: Regular messaging
- **Challenge Completion**: Bonus XP from challenges
- **Voice Activity**: Time in voice channels
- **Reactions**: Reacting to messages
- **Special Events**: XP bonuses during events
